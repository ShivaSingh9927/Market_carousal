## 4. Case Studies & Project Portfolio

### 4.1 MarkoAI (Marketing Automation)
* **Category:** Marketing / Generative AI
* **Solution:** End-to-end AI-powered marketing engine that generates campaign ideas, ad copy, creatives, visuals, captions, and platform-ready posts.
* **Problems Solved:** Slow manual creation, team dependency, inconsistent brand voice.
* **Impact:** 70% reduction in campaign creation time; 30+ assets generated per campaign.

### 4.2 MedAssist AI (Healthcare)
* **Category:** Healthcare / Multimodal AI
* **Solution:** Clinical decision support system combining chest X-ray analysis, pathology detection, OCR-based report processing, and an LLM assistant.
* **Impact:** 40% reduction in diagnostic TAT; 95% model sensitivity.

### 4.3 NHAI NHMMS (Logistics / Gov)
* **Category:** Logistics / Computer Vision
* **Solution:** Nation-scale sanitation monitoring system using AI image scoring and dashboards across 100+ sites.
* **Impact:** Objective cleanliness scoring and improved transparency.

### 4.4 SensAi (Healthcare)
* **Category:** Medical Imaging / Prenatal Care
* **Solution:** AI-powered prenatal ultrasound screening for detecting congenital heart defects and fetal abnormalities.
* **Impact:** 90%+ detection accuracy in pilots; standardized screening workflows.

### 4.5 AIM-N (Automated Intelligent Market Notifier)
* **Category:** Market Intelligence
* **Solution:** Monitors markets using user-defined strategies and sends alerts via WhatsApp and email.
* **Impact:** Real-time alerts and reduced monitoring effort.

### 4.6 Supply Chain Risk Radar
* **Category:** Logistics / Supply Chain
* **Solution:** Scrapes supplier data, filings, and news to compute risk scores and trigger Slack alerts.
* **Impact:** Proactive disruption alerts and improved procurement decisions.

### 4.7 Damage Reporting Assistant
* **Category:** Logistics / Computer Vision
* **Solution:** Vision-based damage reporting tool using Telegram, AI image analysis, and automated HTML reports.
* **Impact:** Reduced report time to <30 seconds.

### 4.8 WhatsApp Support AI
* **Category:** Automation / Conversational AI
* **Solution:** WhatsApp-based support bot connected to a Google Docs knowledge base.
* **Impact:** <5s response time; 24/7 availability.

### 4.9 LinkedIn AutoPilot
* **Category:** Marketing Automation
* **Solution:** Automated content engine that generates posts, designs images, and schedules publishing.
* **Impact:** 120+ posts/month generated; 15+ hours saved weekly.
